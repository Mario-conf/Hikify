The Correcaminos Castell Association is a passionate community of hiking and outdoor activities enthusiasts. Our goal is to foster a connection with nature, promote an active lifestyle, and share enriching experiences with our members. Who are we? We are a diverse group of hiking enthusiasts, from beginners to experts. Our common passion binds us in exploring natural landscapes, discovering scenic routes, and enjoying the company of fellow nature lovers. Our activities:
  • Hiking: We regularly organize excursions through picturesque trails, mountains, and forests. From gentle walks to challenging climbs, there's something for everyone.
  • Outdoor activities: In addition to hiking, we participate in other activities such as cycling, camping, climbing, and bird watching.
  • Community: We believe in camaraderie and mutual support. Our social gatherings and events allow us to share stories, tips, and laughter.
Join us: If you are passionate about nature and want to be part of an active and friendly community, we invite you to join the Correcaminos Castell! Together, we will explore the natural treasures of our region and create lasting memories. For more information and to stay updated on our activities, contact us or visit our official Facebook page.
